How can Gen-AI assist in the complex process of significance assessment? Join us at CAA 2026 for a hands-on experiment with InSites, a dedicated AI assistant for heritage assessment.

In this interactive session, we go beyond the "hype" to explore a Human-AI collaboration workflow. Through creating a Context-Based Significance Assessment (CBSA), we will:

- **Navigate the Assessment Process**: Use specialized mini-agents to transform site documentation into evidence-linked reasoning, from extracting contexts to drafting the final significance statement.
- **Unlock New Representations**: Experiment with optional outputs like Knowledge Graphs, dashboards, and multi-vocal narratives to see your site data in a new light.
- **Explore the InSites AI Design Principles**: Critically look behind the mirror and understand how to design AI that functions as an interpretive enhancement, prioritizing transparency, explainability, and ethics over "magic."

Come ready to explore the potential and challenge the ethics and produce a draft site evaluation you co-authored with InSites.

*The InSites tool, source protocols (GitHub), and workshop website (guidelines & insights) will remain accessible to the participants.
